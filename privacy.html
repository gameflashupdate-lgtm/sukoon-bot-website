Privacy Policy for Sukoon Bot

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

We take your privacy seriously. This document outlines the minimal data Sukoon collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

Sukoon collects and stores the following information:

  • **Server ID (Guild ID):** Used to store server-specific settings (e.g., prefix, welcome message channels, moderation logs).
  • **User IDs:** Used primarily for the leveling system (to track XP) and for moderator action logging (bans, kicks).
  • **Channel IDs:** Used to designate specific channels for bot functions (e.g., music channel, logging channel).

2. Use of Data

The collected data is exclusively used for the proper functioning of the Sukoon bot on Discord. We **do not** share, sell, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside of providing the bot's features.

3. External APIs (Music)

For music functionality, Sukoon interacts with external services (e.g., YouTube). By using the music commands, you acknowledge that these services may have their own data collection policies.

4. Data Removal

Upon the bot being kicked from a server, all associated data (Server ID, settings, level data) is usually removed automatically within 24 hours. If you need manual deletion, please contact the owner (**Space**) through the Support Discord Server.

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please join our Support Discord Server.
